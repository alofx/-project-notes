## Budget Pacing Rules

### First 2 Weeks (Learning Phase)
- Do NOT pause campaigns for low performance
- Let Google gather conversion data
- Only pause keywords with 0 clicks AND $10+ spent

### After 2 Weeks (Optimization Phase)
- If CPA < $30: Increase budget 20%
- If CPA $30-50: Maintain current budget
- If CPA > $50: Review landing page first, then tighten keywords

### Monthly Budget Reallocation
- Move 20% from worst-performing campaign to best-performing
- Example: If Location campaign has CPA $60 but Core has CPA $25, shift $2/day from Location to Core

## Keyword Bidding Strategy at $750

### Manual CPC Starting Bids

| Brand | $2.00 | $3.00 |
| Core Services | $4.00 | $8.00 |
| Location | $3.50 | $7.00 |
| Spanish | $2.50 | $5.00 |

### When to Switch to Automated Bidding
- **Target CPA** (tCPA): After 30 conversions in 30 days
- **Maximize Conversions**: After 20 conversions in 30 days
- **Target ROAS** (tROAS): After 50 conversions with clear value data

> At $750/month, expect to hit 30 conversions by Week 6-8. Switch to tCPA of $35 at that point.

1. Review landing page (likely the issue)
2. Tighten keyword targeting
3. Pause keywords with CPA > $60
4. Add more negative keywords
