## Budget Allocation — Spanish Campaigns

**Option A: Separate Spanish Campaigns**
- Duplicate Campaign 2 (Core Services) → Spanish version, $8/day
- Duplicate Campaign 3 (Location) → Spanish version, $5/day
- Keep English campaigns at reduced budgets

**Option B: Bilingual Ad Groups (Recommended for $750 budget)**
Add Spanish RSAs alongside English RSAs in the same ad groups. Google will automatically serve the right language based on user settings.

## Spanish Landing Page Requirements

If running Spanish ads, you need a Spanish version of your landing page or at minimum:
- Spanish headline + CTA above the fold
- Phone number with Spanish-speaking staff note
- Spanish FAQ section
- "Hablamos Español" badge prominently displayed

**Suggested Spanish CTA:**
- "Reserva Tu Cita — Llama o Mensaje"
- "Cotización Gratis en 60 Segundos"
- "Llama Ahora: 305-853-6101"
